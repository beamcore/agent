About Russia

Russia, officially known as the Russian Federation, is the largest country in the world, spanning two continents: Europe and Asia. It covers an area of approximately 17.1 million square kilometers.

Russian Culture

Russia has a rich cultural heritage that includes literature, ballet, classical music, and visual arts. Famous Russian writers include Leo Tolstoy, Fyodor Dostoevsky, and Anton Chekhov. The Bolshoi and Mariinsky theatres are world-renowned for their ballet performances.

Russian Landmarks

Some of the most famous landmarks in Russia include:

  • The Red Square and Kremlin in Moscow
  • The Hermitage Museum in Saint Petersburg
  • Lake Baikal, the deepest lake in the world
  • The Volga River, the longest river in Europe
